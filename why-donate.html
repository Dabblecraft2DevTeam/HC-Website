Why Donate?

A considerable amount of effort has been invested in this server, reflecting a significant and ongoing commitment from those involved in its development, especially its sole coder. Operating the server incurs costs, and your support can enable the coder to dedicate more time to creating new features.

High Caliber comprises multiple servers. Our public battle servers, ranking system, and in-game currency system are independent of the donation process; this is not a pay-to-win environment. Only cosmetic enhancements may be available in those servers. However, our primary donor perks include:

  • Additional ship plots for building ships and submarines (you start with four free plots and can earn more at no cost)
  • Permissions to use and replicate ships from our official ship registry (a planned feature)
  • Access to the private server, where you will have full ship spawning permissions and some administrative privileges.

Why Are They Called Donations?

One reason for referring to them as donations is that I cannot manage this server as a full-fledged business on my own, providing the same guarantees as a professional organization. Most importantly, this means that, for the most part, donations are non-refundable. Your contributions will likely be spent quickly on server upkeep, and we cannot assure refunds for this reason.

When you donate to High Caliber, please do so because you appreciate the work that has already been accomplished here and, most importantly, wish to see it continue. The donor perks should be viewed as secondary and are subject to change. I will strive to introduce exciting features for donors, but please understand that there are limitations, and plans may evolve.
